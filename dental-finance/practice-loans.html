Practice Loans – Funding Options for UK Dentists

Comprehensive guidance on securing practice loans, equipment finance, and funding options tailored for UK dental practice owners.

Why Practice Loans Matter

Practice loans are essential for dentists looking to buy, expand, or refurbish a clinic. Lenders consider NHS contract values, private income, and associate arrangements, rather than generic business lending criteria.

Types of Practice Loans

From acquisition finance to equipment or refurbishment loans, understanding the lending options helps dentists secure the most suitable funding. Our guidance explains loan structures, repayment terms, and lender considerations.

Funding Considerations

Loan approval often depends on stable cash flow, profitability, and professional indemnity arrangements. Tailored dental finance ensures lenders assess practice-specific factors accurately, protecting personal and practice assets.

How to Choose the Right Loan

Evaluating interest rates, repayment schedules, and lender flexibility is key. DentalLab.co.uk provides independent insight to help practice owners make informed decisions without sales pressure.

Legal & Financial Safeguards

Loan agreements intersect with employment and indemnity contracts. Ensure legal compliance and risk mitigation through proper structuring. See Dental Legal for related guidance.
