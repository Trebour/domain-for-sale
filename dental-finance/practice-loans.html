Practice Loans – Funding Guidance for UK Dentists

Comprehensive guidance on securing practice loans, equipment finance, and funding options tailored for UK dental practice owners.

Why Practice Loans Are Critical

Practice loans provide dentists with the capital needed to acquire, refurbish, or expand a clinic. Lenders assess NHS contract value, private income, and associate arrangements rather than traditional SME lending criteria. Choosing the right loan ensures financial stability and supports sustainable growth.

Types of Practice Loans

Understanding different types of loans helps you select the best fit for your practice:

  • Acquisition Loans – for purchasing a new practice
  • Refurbishment Loans – for modernizing facilities
  • Equipment Finance – for CAD/CAM and other essential equipment
  • Structured Loans – flexible repayment terms tailored to cash flow

Funding Considerations

Approval often depends on stable cash flow, profitability, and professional indemnity arrangements. Lenders focus on dental-specific criteria to safeguard both personal and practice assets. Key factors include:

  • Current revenue streams (NHS vs private)
  • Associate arrangements
  • Existing debts and liabilities
  • Professional indemnity coverage
