Professional Indemnity

Indemnity cover protects dentists from claims related to clinical negligence or professional advice. Key points include:

  • Assessing policy limits and exclusions
  • Coverage for associates and practice staff
  • Claims handling and legal support

For overlap with GDC investigations and legal compliance, see Dental Legal and Dental Indemnity.

Practice Insurance

Practice insurance safeguards your physical and operational assets. Consider:

  • Buildings and contents insurance
  • Business interruption cover
  • Equipment and technology protection, including CAD/CAM systems

Learn more about protecting equipment in Dental Equipment & Services.

Tax Planning for Dentists

Efficient tax planning helps dentists retain more profit while remaining compliant. Considerations include:

  • Optimizing business structure (sole trader, partnership, limited company)
  • Claiming allowable business expenses
  • Pension contributions and income timing

Strategic planning can be integrated with broader business finance guidance in Business Finance and lending decisions in Practice Loans.

Risk Management & Compliance

Financial and insurance decisions are intertwined with regulatory obligations. Ensure:

  • Compliance with GDC and CQC regulations
  • Legal protection for associates and staff
  • Proper documentation of coverage and contracts

For detailed legal guidance, see Regulatory Compliance.
