Dental Equipment Finance – Investing in Modern Dentistry

Funding solutions for UK dentists to acquire CAD/CAM systems, imaging devices, and essential clinical equipment to maintain high standards of care.

Understanding Dental Equipment Finance

Dental equipment finance allows practices to acquire essential technology without large upfront costs. Options include leasing, hire purchase, and structured loans tailored for UK dentists.

Financing Options

  • Lease agreements for short-term or flexible access
  • Hire purchase for eventual ownership of equipment
  • Structured loans integrated with practice finances (Practice Loans Overview)

Types of Equipment

Practices can finance:

  • CAD/CAM systems (CAD/CAM & Lab Equipment Investment)
  • Imaging equipment, X-ray units, and scanners
  • Dental chairs, autoclaves, and other essential clinical tools
