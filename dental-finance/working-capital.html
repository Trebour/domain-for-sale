Working Capital Strategies for UK Dentists

Learn how dental practices can maintain liquidity, manage short-term obligations, and ensure smooth operations while planning for growth.

Understanding Working Capital

Working capital is the difference between your current assets and liabilities. Managing it effectively ensures your practice can cover day-to-day expenses while funding new opportunities.

Optimizing Receivables

  • Invoice patients promptly and accurately
  • Track outstanding balances and follow up efficiently
  • Consider digital payment options to speed cash inflow

Managing Payables

  • Negotiate payment terms with suppliers
  • Schedule payments to optimize cash on hand
  • Monitor recurring expenses for cost-saving opportunities

Maintaining Liquidity Buffers

Keep contingency funds to cover unexpected costs, seasonal fluctuations, or temporary revenue drops. This reduces financial stress and improves decision-making confidence.
