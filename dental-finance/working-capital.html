Working Capital & Operational Finance

Strategies to manage working capital, control expenses, and ensure your UK dental practice operates efficiently every day.

Optimizing Working Capital

Working capital ensures your practice has sufficient liquidity to meet short-term obligations. Key tactics include:

  • Monitoring receivables and payables to avoid cash shortages
  • Maintaining contingency reserves for emergencies
  • Using short-term financing only when necessary

Operational Finance Strategies

Day-to-day financial decisions impact efficiency and profitability. Focus on:

  • Staff scheduling and payroll efficiency
  • Supplier agreements and purchasing cycles
  • Tracking operational metrics through financial reporting tools
  • Managing insurance premiums and indemnity costs strategically

Aligning Operations with Growth

Operational finance supports strategic expansion. Coordinating working capital with investment, equipment upgrades, and loan repayments ensures smooth growth without overextending resources.
