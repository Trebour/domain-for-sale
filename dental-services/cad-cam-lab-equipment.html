Why Invest in CAD/CAM & Lab Equipment

Digital dentistry has transformed modern practice. Investing in CAD/CAM and lab equipment enables:

  • Faster, more accurate restorations and prosthetics
  • Reduced turnaround times for patients
  • Integration with practice management software and digital workflows
  • Increased autonomy for in-house labs

Understanding the technology and choosing the right equipment ensures a balance between capital expenditure and clinical benefit.

Key CAD/CAM Equipment

Core systems include:

  • Intraoral scanners for digital impressions
  • CAD design software for crowns, bridges, and orthodontics
  • Milling machines for precision restorations
  • 3D printers for model production and surgical guides

Lab Equipment & In-House Solutions

Having an in-house lab requires:

  • Investment in digital and manual lab equipment
  • Skilled technicians trained in CAD/CAM workflows
  • Software integration for seamless case management
  • Maintenance and calibration to ensure high quality output

Many practices combine in-house capabilities with external labs for flexibility and efficiency.
