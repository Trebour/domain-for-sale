CAD/CAM & Digital Dentistry

Explore CAD/CAM systems, digital workflows, and software solutions that streamline restorative dentistry, improve lab communication, and enhance treatment accuracy for UK dental practices.

Overview

CAD/CAM (Computer-Aided Design and Manufacturing) and digital dentistry technologies are transforming how dental practices deliver restorations. By integrating digital impression systems, design software, and lab communication tools, dental teams can achieve greater precision, reduce turnaround times, and improve patient satisfaction.

Key CAD/CAM Software Features

  • Digital Impression Systems: Capture high-resolution 3D scans of teeth and arches for accurate restoration design.
  • Restorative Design Software: Design crowns, bridges, veneers, and implants with precision and automation tools.
  • Lab Communication Tools: Seamless digital transfer of designs, modifications, and patient data to dental labs.
  • Integration with Practice Management: Connect CAD/CAM workflows with patient records, scheduling, and billing systems.
  • 3D Printing & Milling Support: Prepare digital files for manufacturing, ensuring accurate restorations and reduced remakes.

Benefits of Digital Dentistry

  • Enhanced precision and fit of restorations
  • Faster turnaround times from clinic to lab
  • Improved patient experience and reduced chair time
  • Better collaboration between dentist, staff, and lab technicians
  • Streamlined record keeping and compliance with UK regulations

Choosing the Right CAD/CAM Solution

When evaluating CAD/CAM and digital dentistry systems, consider:

  • Compatibility with existing imaging and practice management software
  • User-friendliness and staff training requirements
  • Type of restoration support (crowns, bridges, implants, veneers)
  • Integration with labs and manufacturing equipment
  • Software updates, support, and scalability for practice growth
