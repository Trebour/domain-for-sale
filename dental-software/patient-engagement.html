Patient Engagement & Telehealth

Explore dental software solutions that improve patient engagement, enable online consultations, and support remote care for UK dental practices. Enhance communication, appointment management, and overall patient experience with digital tools.

Overview

Patient engagement and telehealth software are transforming dental practice-patient interactions. From online booking and automated reminders to video consultations and digital education, these tools enhance accessibility, satisfaction, and clinical efficiency.

Core Features

  • Online Appointment Scheduling: Web/mobile booking, automated confirmations, and reminders.
  • Teleconsultation Platforms: Secure video consultations for diagnosis, follow-ups, and triage.
  • Patient Communication: SMS/email notifications, recall reminders, treatment plan updates, and education delivery.
  • Digital Consent & Records: Electronic consent forms integrated with patient records.
  • Feedback & Surveys: Collect feedback, monitor satisfaction, and improve services.

Benefits for Practices

  • Reduce missed appointments and improve clinic efficiency
  • Enhance patient experience and communication
  • Support remote care and triage
  • Integrate seamlessly with practice management systems
  • Improve treatment compliance and patient education
