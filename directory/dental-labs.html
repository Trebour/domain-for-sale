UK Dental Laboratories Directory

A curated directory of dental laboratories across the UK supporting practices with restorative, cosmetic, orthodontic, implant, and digital dental services.

About UK Dental Laboratories

Dental laboratories play a critical role in modern dentistry, working closely with practices to manufacture crowns, bridges, dentures, implants, orthodontic appliances, and digital restorations. This directory provides an overview of established UK dental laboratories operating across traditional, digital, and hybrid workflows.

Rather than rankings or reviews, this page helps dental professionals discover laboratory partners based on services, workflow types, and regional coverage.

Types of Dental Laboratories

  • Traditional laboratories specialising in manual craftsmanship
  • Digital dental labs using CAD/CAM and 3D printing
  • Hybrid laboratories combining digital design and manual finishing
  • Specialist labs focused on implants, orthodontics, or cosmetic workflows
