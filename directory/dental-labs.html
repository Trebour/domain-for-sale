UK Dental Laboratories Directory

A curated directory of dental laboratories across the UK, supporting practices with restorative, cosmetic, orthodontic, and implant-based laboratory services.

About UK Dental Laboratories

Dental laboratories play a critical role in modern dentistry, working closely with practices to manufacture crowns, bridges, dentures, implants, orthodontic appliances, and digitally designed restorations. This directory provides an overview of established UK dental laboratories operating across traditional, digital, and hybrid workflows.

Rather than rankings or reviews, this page is designed to support discovery and research by listing laboratories based on service scope, workflow type, and regional coverage.

Types of Dental Laboratories

  • Traditional laboratories specialising in manual craftsmanship
  • Digital dental labs using CAD/CAM and digital impressions
  • Hybrid laboratories combining digital design with hand-finishing
  • Specialist labs focused on implants, orthodontics, or cosmetic work
