Explore a curated list of UK dental milling centres and CAD/CAM labs providing high-quality prosthetic and implant components to dental practices and laboratories.

About Dental Milling Centres

Dental milling centres specialise in precision manufacturing of crowns, bridges, implant abutments, dentures, and full-arch prosthetics using CAD/CAM technology. These centres serve both dental laboratories and clinics seeking outsourced production.

The directory includes digital-only labs, hybrid milling centres, and full-service facilities offering rapid turnaround and high-accuracy solutions for modern dentistry.

Types of Milling Centres

  • CAD/CAM-focused centres using 3D scanning, milling, and printing
  • Hybrid centres combining digital milling with manual finishing
  • Implant-specialist centres producing abutments and full-arch prosthetics
  • Full-service production centres supporting labs and clinics nationwide
