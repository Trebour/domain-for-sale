UK Dental Milling Centres & CAD/CAM Production Directory

A curated directory of UK dental milling centres, CAD/CAM production facilities, and digital prosthetic service partners supporting practices and laboratories.

About Dental Milling Centres

Dental milling centres provide specialised CAD/CAM production services for crowns, bridges, implant components, dentures, and other prosthetic solutions. These facilities work with digital files from practices and labs to deliver precision-manufactured restorations using milling machines, 3D printers, and related digital equipment.

This directory lists milling centres and production facilities in the UK to help dental professionals identify partners for outsourcing manufacturing and rapid digital production.
