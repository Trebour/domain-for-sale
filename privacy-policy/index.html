Privacy Policy – Protecting Your Data

DentalLab.co.uk respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This page explains how we collect, use, and safeguard data for our users, including dentists, practice owners, and site visitors in the UK.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better experience and to improve the services we offer. Types of data collected include:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, or other contact details submitted via forms or newsletter signups.
  • Non-Personal Information: Browser type, device information, pages visited, and time spent on the site.
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies to improve user experience, analyze site traffic, and display relevant ads.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small files stored on your device to enhance site performance. We use cookies to:

  • Remember preferences and login details.
  • Analyze visitor behavior for site optimization.
  • Deliver relevant advertisements via Google AdSense.

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings; however, some site functionality may be affected.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared with trusted third parties under strict confidentiality for:

  • Website hosting and maintenance services.
  • Analytics and advertising platforms, including Google Analytics and AdSense.
  • Legal compliance and regulatory obligations.

All third-party services comply with GDPR and UK data protection regulations.

Your Rights Under GDPR

As a user in the UK, you have the following rights under the GDPR and UK Data Protection Act:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data we hold.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccuracies in your data.
  • Deletion: Request that your data be erased where legally permissible.
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing, including marketing communications.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via our contact page.
