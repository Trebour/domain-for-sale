Privacy Policy – Protecting Your Data

DentalLab.co.uk respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This page explains how we collect, use, and safeguard data for our users, including dentists, practice owners, and site visitors in the UK.

Information We Collect

DentalLab.co.uk collects information to provide a reliable, secure, and informative experience for UK dental professionals. The data we collect may include:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, or other details voluntarily submitted via contact forms or enquiries.
  • Non-Personal Information: Browser type, device information, IP address, pages visited, and time spent on the site.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Used to improve functionality, analyse traffic, and support advertising services.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Responding to enquiries and providing requested information.
  • Improving website performance, content quality, and user experience.
  • Monitoring site security and preventing fraud.
  • Delivering relevant advertising in accordance with applicable laws.
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations.

Cookies, Google AdSense & Advertising

DentalLab.co.uk uses cookies to enhance user experience and support advertising services.

We use Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service provided by Google. Google uses cookies, including the DoubleClick cookie, to serve ads to users based on their visits to this and other websites.

The DoubleClick cookie enables Google and its partners to serve interest-based advertisements. Users may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • www.aboutads.info

Cookies may also be used for analytics, remembering preferences, and improving overall site functionality. You can disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some features of the site may not function correctly.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to operate and improve our website, including:

  • Google Analytics for traffic analysis
  • Google AdSense for advertising
  • Website hosting and security providers

These third parties may collect, store, and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable data protection laws.

Your Rights Under UK GDPR

If you are located in the UK or EEA, you have rights under the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act, including:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of data where legally permissible
  • The right to object to processing for marketing purposes
  • The right to data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us via our contact page.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational safeguards to protect your information, including secure servers, HTTPS encryption, and restricted access controls. While we take reasonable steps to protect data, no online transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children’s Information

DentalLab.co.uk is intended for professional users and is not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
